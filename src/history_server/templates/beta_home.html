Record All the history

A simple local agent that records all your bash history, and then pushes the data to the Save History app. So you have a record of all your history, from all your terminals all in one place.

Easy setup. Unixy goodness..

What gets recorded is what goes into your history file. If you dont want duplicates, configure that locally. Dont want to wait until you exit the shell to record the commands, configure it locally. Easy local setup.
